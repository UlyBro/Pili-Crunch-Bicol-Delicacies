About Us

Pili Crunch – Bicol Delicacies

Type of Business: Pili Nuts / Manufacturing

Business Owner: John Leonard Tobello, Cherie Mae Jacob

During our Business Plan Defense, we decided to innovate an existing process that needed improvements to penetrate the market. We discovered the potential of the Pili Business — a true gem from Bicol that can grow bigger with the right innovation and heart.

After graduation, we started our dry runs in Cavite, and to our surprise, it turned out successful. This inspired us to continue improving our process and products. Today, we’re proud to operate Pili Crunch with gradual improvements and consistent results, bringing authentic Bicolano flavor to every Filipino home.

“Passion. Pride. Pili.”
